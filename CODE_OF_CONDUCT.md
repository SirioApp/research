# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent, but discuss ideas rigorously.
- Keep feedback actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or publishing private/confidential data.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting
Please report conduct issues to:
- conduct@backedresearch.io
